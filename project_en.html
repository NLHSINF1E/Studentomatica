Project

During project you and your group made of a couple of students from the same education area recieve a couple of different projects you need to complete. One of these projects for example could be to program a complete website. During projecthours at school you can apply the different new lesson topics on the wesite of you and your projectgroup. Project is a good way to test the skills of you and your group.
