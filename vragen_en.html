Frequently Asked Questions

On this page you can find frequently asked questions that we try to answer for you here as well as possible.

Q: Where can I go for questions to the teacher?

A: It is best to send the relevant teacher an e-mail with your question, the e-mail addresses of all teachers can be found on the "Teachers" page.

Q: Where can I find my grades?

A: Since this is a student platform for students, teachers and parents, we are not allowed to show students' grades, these figures can be found on the NHL Stenden website progress.

Q: Is there also an English version of the student platform for international students?

A: Yes, you can find this in the main menu at the top of the site, under the heading "NL" it is possible to switch to the English-language website.

Q: When are the test weeks?

A: This information can be found on our website under the heading "annual review".

Q: How can I register for a study program at NHL Stenden?

A: For this you can go to the website of the NHL Stenden, there you can find a course that suits you and you can register for this course right away by clicking the button "register for this course".
