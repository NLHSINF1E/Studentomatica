Informationmanagement

Information management is the responsibility for maintenance and the use of documents, even as capturing and maintaining the information about businessactivities. Information management is a process that takes care of the different needs of information from different branches of an organisation. An information manager is a client of a ICT supplier. An information manager isn't a part of an ict department, but of an user organisation. Organisations need to constantly adapt themselves to the changes in their environment, by making good use of the information you can properly anticipate to the changes. Employees of the organisation need to get acces quickly and easily to information so they can do their job properly.
