SCG (study career guidance)

Together with your group you get a SGC, a study career guide. This is a teachter appointed to you and your group by your school, this persone keeps track of the progress you make with your group and makes sure everything runs smoothly. Also most of the times this person is the point of contact and you can go to this person when troubles occur.
