Study Advice Computer Science

The computer science NHL Stenden course in Emmen is a full-time course that specializes in coding/programming, network management, and/or IT security. NHL Stenden also offers other study variants of the computer science program, such as an associate degree and a branch of computer science, technical computer science available.

AD IT-Management

As a study variant of Computer Science, an AD (associate degree) IT management course is also available at NHL Stenden. In this two-year course, the basics of coding and programming are taught in the first year, after which the specific IT management is discussed in the second year. In the course, you will be trained to program safely and efficiently. In the end, you will eventually become a true IT professional..

Technical Computer Science

As a study variant of Computer Science, an AD (associate degree) IT management course is also available at NHL Stenden. Interships Computer Science

In the bachelor's degree in Computer Science, students go on an internship in the 3rd year, where students can join a company where they will work for a period of at least six months. The company may decide to keep the student for a longer period of time for payment, this will often lead to a final job in this company or other similar companies. In the AD IT Management course, students go on an internship for six months in the second year, this is done in the same way as for the bachelor's.
