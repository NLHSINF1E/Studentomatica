Welcome to the student platform "StudentOmatica"

The student platform of NHL Stenden: StudentOmatica is a student platform by and for students. Studentomatica advises, guides and supports students and pupils. On this platform, students, teachers and others can find all information related to their training. Student platform Studentomatica has been developed to make student life easier for students.

Latest News

Corona update - October 14, 2020

On Tuesday evening, October 13, the government announced new corona measures. The impact of this on our education is small; fortunately many educational activities can still continue. But the impact on our life outside of school is significant. Go out for dinner, catch up with friends in a cafe and let off steam on the sports field; it is not possible at all. We are very disappointed with this, but we also understand that it is necessary to get the corona virus under control. We would like to urge everyone to continue to take his or her responsibility in this seriously.

Ad top talents NHL Stenden

Since January 2018, the Associate degree (Ad) has been an independent higher professional education program at level 5. Yet the Ad program is still very unknown. It is high time to pay more attention to this level of education and to put the Ad students at NHL Stenden in the limelight. Twelve Ad students have been nominated by their study program and employer as top Ad talent. On Thursday, October 22, three of them were nominated for the national Ad top talent prize by Executive Board member Oscar Couwenberg.
